About

SCAIA (Sentinel based Complex Agri-Industrial Application) is an operational, automated processing chain, generating customized agricultural products from Sentinel-1 (A&B) and Sentinel-2 (A&B) time series data, according to the client’s predefined requests. The Web application was tailored to the needs of Dalmand Corporation (Bonafarm Group), and provides near real time monitoring of their cropland management. The standalone system can be run on any hardware running a Unix-like operating system; even on HPCs and cloud computing server infrastructures. The system is currently configured to develop 42 dedicated products under 4 main categories. Most of the final products are related to four major crop types (winter wheat, winter barley, rapeseed, and corn). All services were requested by our pilot partner, and each product was verified by experts in respect to their own operations purposes. SCAIA is a joint effort of Sarmap SA, Dalmand Corporation, and the University of Pécs. The development was supported by the European Space Agency and the National Information Infrastructure Development Institute supercomputing infrastructure (NIIF HPC).
